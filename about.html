About YourHelpa

Our mission is simple: to eliminate payment risk and unreliable service in the Nigerian local economy.

From the Field: Why We Built the Escrow System.

The story of YourHelpa begins not in a boardroom, but on the ground, across cities like Lagos, Owerri, Osogbo, and Ibadan, and even extending to Ghana and Ivory Coast. Our founder, Prince Dike, spent years as a multi-skilled artisan and technician. He has mastered trades including:

  • Painting, Plumbing, & Home Decorations
  • Graphic Design & UI/UX Design
  • Web/Software Development & Tech Product Management
  • Marketing, Copywriting, & Education

Having been both the provider (a "Helpa") and the consumer, he intimately understood the deep-seated frustrations of the local service economy: the pain of completing a job only to be left unpaid, and the equal struggle of finding a reliable, verified professional you can trust. This dual experience revealed a critical gap. The problem demanded a guaranteed solution that was accessible to everyone—which led directly to the creation of the YourHelpa WhatsApp Chatbot and its core Escrow system.

The Dual Advantage: Field Wisdom Meets AI Power.

Prince's journey didn't stop in the field. His transition into high-level tech product management and software development provided the final piece of the puzzle. He was now equipped not just to understand the problem, but to build the solution.

YourHelpa is not just another app. It's an intelligent system designed by someone who knows exactly what questions to ask and what securities to provide. We leverage AI for smart matching, automated verification to build a trusted community, and robust Escrow logic to manage financial risk, ensuring that both the Helpa's work and the consumer's money are fully protected.
