Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We're here to help.

What is YourHelpa?

YourHelpa is a platform that connects you to trusted service providers (Helpas) and product vendors through WhatsApp. You can request a service, get quotes, and pay securely, all within a single chat conversation.

Is it free to request a service?

Yes, it is completely free to make a request and get quotes from Helpas. You only pay for the service or product itself once you agree to the terms.

How do I pay for a service?

All payments are handled securely through our platform. We will provide you with a secure payment link in your WhatsApp chat. We hold the payment until the task is completed to ensure your satisfaction.

How do you vet your Helpas?

Every Helpa on our platform goes through a verification process, which includes identity checks and skill validation where applicable. We are committed to building a community of trusted and reliable professionals.
