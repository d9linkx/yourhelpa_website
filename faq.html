Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform.

General Questions

What is YourHelpa?

YourHelpa is a trusted community marketplace that connects people who need to outsource tasks with skilled, local individuals (Helpas) who are ready to help.

How do I post a task?

Simply click "Sign Up," create an account, and follow the prompts to post your task. You'll describe what you need, suggest a fair budget, and post it for our community of Helpas to see.

Is it free to post a task?

Yes, it is completely free to post a task on YourHelpa. You only pay for the task once it's completed to your satisfaction.

For Helpas

How do I start working as a Helpa?

Click "Become a Helpa" and sign up. You'll build your profile, set your skills and rates, and go through a verification process. Once approved, you can start making offers on tasks in your area.

How do I get paid?

Once a task is marked as complete by the customer, payment is securely transferred to your YourHelpa account. You can then withdraw your earnings to your bank account.

Can I set my own schedule and rates?

Absolutely. As a Helpa, you are your own boss. You choose which tasks to make offers on, and you set the rates for your services. Work as much or as little as you like.
