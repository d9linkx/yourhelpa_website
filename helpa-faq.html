Helpa Questions

Your guide to succeeding on the YourHelpa platform.

How and when do I get paid?

Once a customer confirms the job is complete via WhatsApp, our system automatically processes your payment. The funds (your agreed price minus the 7% commission) are transferred to your registered bank account instantly.

What are the fees for being a Helpa?

Helpas subscribe for ₦1,000 per month to get access to verified, pre-paid job leads. We also charge a 7% commission on the final price of each completed job. This commission funds the secure Escrow system, guaranteeing you always get paid.

What if a customer cancels a job?

If a customer cancels before you have started any work, there is no fee or penalty. If a dispute arises after work has begun, our support team will mediate to ensure a fair outcome for both parties based on the work completed.
