The YourHelpa Pro Guide

Maximize your earnings, deliver exceptional service, and build an outstanding reputation on the YourHelpa platform.

Welcome to the official guide for every successful Helpa! This page contains actionable tips to help you win more jobs, earn higher ratings, and maximize your income. We update this guide regularly, so check back often.

1. Build a Stellar Profile

Your profile is your storefront. A complete, professional, and engaging profile is the single most effective tool for winning jobs and setting a premium rate.

1.1 Crafting a Winning Bio

  • Be Clear & Concise: Immediately state what you offer (e.g., "Experienced plumber for emergency repairs").
  • Highlight Value: Mention certifications, speed, or special equipment you use.
  • Set Expectations: Briefly describe your process, reliability, and commitment to satisfaction.
  • Professional Tone: Maintain a friendly yet confident and reliable tone.

1.2 Optimize Skills & Categories

  • Be Specific: Instead of "Cleaning," list "Deep Residential Cleaning," "Move-out Cleaning," etc.
  • Use Keywords: Match your skills to the terms customers are searching for.
  • Show Certifications: Upload verifiable licenses to significantly increase trust.

1.3 Professional Photography

  • Profile Picture: A clear, well-lit, smiling headshot.
  • Portfolio: High-quality photos of your best work (e.g., Before/After shots).
  • Show Your Tools: Photos of professional equipment build confidence.

1.4 Setting Your Rate

  • Market Research: Check rates of other top-rated Helpas in your area.
  • Value-Based Pricing: Price based on your expertise, not just time. Specialists can charge more.
  • Be Transparent: Clearly define what your rate includes to avoid surprises.

2. Master Your Communication

Effective, timely, and polite communication is crucial for converting inquiries into booked jobs and earning high ratings.

2.1 Responding to Inquiries

  • Speed is Key: Aim to respond within 30 minutes to secure the job.
  • Personalize: Use templates but always address the customer by name and reference their request.
  • Clarify: Ask questions to fully understand the job scope before quoting.

2.2 Providing Accurate Quotes

  • Stick to Your Quote: Only adjust the price if the job scope changes, and always get customer approval first.
  • Break it Down: If applicable, list costs for materials, labor, etc., for transparency.
  • Confirm Details: Restate the time, date, location, and total price before finalizing.

2.3 Professionalism in Chat

  • Good Etiquette: Use complete sentences, correct grammar, and polite language.
  • Keep it on Platform: All communication must stay on YourHelpa to ensure you're protected.

3. Perfect Your Job Execution

Executing a job successfully is about efficiency, attention to detail, and a focus on the customer experience.

3.1 Pre-Job Preparation

  • Confirm: Send a confirmation message 24 hours before the job.
  • Be Punctual: Plan your route. If you'll be late, communicate immediately.

3.2 The Service Delivery

  • Initial Check-in: Briefly review the job scope with the customer before starting.
  • Quality & Efficiency: Deliver high-quality results and always leave the work area clean and tidy.
  • Final Walkthrough: Ask the customer to review the work before you finish to ensure 100% satisfaction.

3.3 Driving 5-Star Reviews

Your reputation is everything. High ratings drive future bookings and higher earnings.

  • Politely Ask: After a successful job, ask the customer to leave a review on the platform.
  • Respond to All Reviews: Thank positive reviewers. Address negative reviews professionally and offer solutions.

3.4 Payouts and Platform Fees

  • Invoice Promptly: Submit your invoice via the platform as soon as the job is done.
  • Instant Payout: Funds are transferred to you the moment a customer confirms job completion.
  • Our Fees: A 7% commission per job plus a ₦1,000/month subscription. This covers your payment guarantee, verified leads, and platform support.

4. Stay Safe & Compliant

Insurance

Understand the coverage provided by YourHelpa’s Helpa Protection Policy, which typically covers damage to customer property or injuries sustained during the job.

Local Regulations

You are responsible for complying with all local, state, and national laws, including licensing, taxes, and permits related to the services you offer.

Privacy

Never share customer contact information or personal details outside of the YourHelpa platform to protect both yourself and the customer.
