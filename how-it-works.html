The Zero-Risk Transaction: How YourHelpa Works.

From request to guaranteed payout, the entire process happens in 5 simple steps, all within your WhatsApp.

If You Need a Service (The Buyer's Journey)

1

Request Service

Send a WhatsApp message to our AI chatbot describing the product or service you need.

2

Find Nearby Helpa

The chatbot instantly finds and presents you with a list of verified sellers/providers near you.

3

Choose & Pay

You select your preferred Helpa from the list and pay the agreed amount into the system.

4

Payment Locked

YourHelpa securely locks your payment in Escrow. The Helpa is notified to begin the work.

5

Release on Satisfaction

Once the job is done, you confirm satisfaction. We then instantly release the payment to the Helpa.

If You Provide a Service (The Helpa's Secure Payout)

1

Get Matched

Our AI matches you with nearby customer requests that fit your skills and service offerings.

2

Customer Chooses You

The customer reviews your profile and selects you for the job. You are notified instantly.

3

Payment Secured

You get a notification that the customer's payment is locked in Escrow. It's safe to start work.

4

Deliver Service

Complete the service with confidence, knowing your payment is guaranteed and waiting for you.

5

Get Instant Payout

As soon as the customer confirms satisfaction, your payment (minus commission) is released to you.

Why Escrow is the Game Changer

  • Buyer Protection: Funds only release upon your explicit, written confirmation via WhatsApp.
  • Seller Protection: Ensures funds are available before work starts, eliminating the risk of fraud or default.
  • Dispute Resolution: YourHelpa fairly mediates disputes using the immutable WhatsApp chat history as evidence.
