When you hire a Helpa, you pay the agreed amount to YourHelpa. We hold the funds securely. Only when you confirm on WhatsApp that the job is complete to your satisfaction do we release the payment to the Helpa. This eliminates risk for everyone.

You can find a wide range of services, from home repairs (plumbing, electrical) and cleaning to professional services like tutoring and event planning, as well as product sourcing and delivery. If you need it, a Helpa can probably do it.

Where is YourHelpa available?

YourHelpa is available nationwide across Nigeria. We connect you with trusted Helpas in your local area, wherever you are in the country.

What are the fees for being a Helpa?

Helpas subscribe for ₦1,000 per month to get access to verified, pre-paid job leads. We also charge a 7% commission on the final price of each completed job. This commission funds the secure Escrow system, guaranteeing you always get paid.
