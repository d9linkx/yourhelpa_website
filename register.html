Helpa Application FAQs

How long does the verification process take?

Our verification process is fast and simple. Once you submit your details, it typically takes between 24 to 48 hours for our team to review and approve your profile.

What documents do I need to provide?

We require a valid means of identification (like a National ID, Driver's License, or Voter's Card) and basic information about your skills and services to ensure the safety and trust of our community.

When is the ₦1,000 monthly fee deducted?

Your subscription is billed monthly from the date you are approved. You will receive a notification on WhatsApp to make the payment to keep your account active and continue receiving leads.

If a job is canceled, do I pay the 7% fee?

No. The 7% commission is only charged on successfully completed and confirmed jobs. If a job is canceled by the customer before you start, there is no charge to you.

How quickly are my earnings transferred after job completion?

Instantly. As soon as the customer confirms the job is done via WhatsApp, our system automatically processes and sends your payment to your registered bank account.

Can I pause my subscription?

Yes, you can choose not to renew your monthly subscription if you plan to be unavailable. You can easily reactivate it when you're ready to start receiving job leads again.
