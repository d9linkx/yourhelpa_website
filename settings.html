Documents & Vetting

Your profile must be fully vetted before you can accept job requests. Please upload clear, readable copies of the documents below.

Government ID

A valid, government-issued identification document is required to verify your identity. This builds trust with customers.

Accepted Documents:

  • National ID Card (NIN Slip or Plastic Card)
  • International Passport
  • Driver's License
  • Permanent Voter's Card (PVC)

Note: Ensure the name on your ID matches your profile name.
